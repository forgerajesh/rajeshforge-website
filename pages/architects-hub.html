Eligibility & Selection Criteria

Minimum Eligibility

  • 7+ years relevant architecture or lead implementation experience
  • Hands-on delivery evidence (case outcomes, implementation depth)
  • Ability to work in high-urgency enterprise environments
  • Clear written and verbal communication

Evaluation Focus

  • Problem-solving depth and execution maturity
  • Domain fit (QA/TestOps/AI/Product/Platform)
  • Governance and reliability mindset
  • Client readiness and onboarding speed
